# Use Case Playbook Builder

This skill creates actionable playbooks that enable teams to execute repeatable, winning strategies for specific use cases.

## What Is a Use Case Playbook?

A playbook documents:
- **Who** the ideal customer is for this use case
- **What** pain points and outcomes matter to them
- **How** to identify opportunities and position your solution
- **Why** customers choose you vs. alternatives
- **When** to use which assets and tactics

## Creating Your Playbook

**Start with customer conversations:**
- Interview 3-5 customers who fit this use case
- Document their language, pain points, decision process
- Identify patterns in what matters to them

**Validate with sales:**
- Review with reps who have closed deals in this use case
- Test discovery questions in real conversations
- Refine based on what's actually working

**Launch and iterate:**
- Start with 70% complete, get it in use
- Gather feedback from sales team
- Update monthly based on learnings

**Make it actionable:**
- Clear enough for new rep to use
- Specific enough to guide decisions
- Flexible enough to adapt to specific situations
